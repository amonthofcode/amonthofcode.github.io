About - Why did I create this thing

Well, there are lots of reasons why I want to create this. But this reason is the most important. First, I am a self-learn student. I was interested in Web Development, I learned HTML, CSS, Javascript. But it like I can learn are of these stuff in one month instead of 6 months. Really! I need 6 months to learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript! So I want to create this challenge to let everyone learn code and have a path to learn, no need to gather for resources!. You do learn and do projects (related to code) for every single day in the month. You will get points for your projects. At the end of the month who have the most point wins!

Well, in every challenge (or game), we have rules

1. Code and learn minimum 1 hour a day

2. Make at least 3 projects a month

3. No copy codes from other people (you can still ask for help)

4 You can take a day break (Just 2 day break total for a week)

5. Your points will just be count if you do projects

6. Your points will be graded by everyone in the community
